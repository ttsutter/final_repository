Good Boys is a 2019 American coming-of-age, comedy film, directed by Gene Stupnitsky, in his directorial debut, and written by Stupnitsky and Lee Eisenberg. It stars Jacob Tremblay, Keith L. Williams, and Brady Noon as three sixth-graders who find themselves in a series of misadventures as they try to attend a party hosted by their popular classmates.

Good Boys is avaliable to stream on Amazon, also avaliable for purchase on the Microsoft and Google Play Store.
